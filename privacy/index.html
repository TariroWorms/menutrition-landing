Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the MeNutrition mobile application (the “App”) collects, uses, and shares information. MeNutrition helps you read restaurant menus and home-cooked meals and track your nutrition. We do not sell your personal data.

Information you provide

Your profile (such as age, height, weight, goals, dietary preferences and allergens), the meals you log, your weight history, and any photos you save are stored on your device. If you sign in with Apple or Google, this data can also be backed up to your private account so it survives a reinstall or new phone. This information is used only to power the App’s features for you. It is never shared with advertisers or data brokers.

Menu & food photos

When you scan a menu or a meal, the image is sent to our AI provider (OpenAI) to recognize dishes and estimate nutrition. Photos are processed to return results and are not used to identify you. See OpenAI’s privacy policy.

Approximate location

If you grant location access, we use your approximate area to add local context to menus (currency, regional dishes) and to label history entries. You can deny or revoke this in your device Settings at any time; the App keeps working without it.

Analytics & tracking

We use Firebase Analytics to understand which features are used, in aggregate and anonymously. We never collect this data until you allow tracking through the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt. If you choose “Ask App Not to Track,” analytics collection stays off. The App also includes the Google AdMob SDK; any advertising identifier (IDFA) is only accessed if you grant tracking permission. We do not link analytics to your identity or share it for cross-app advertising. See Firebase/Google privacy.

Subscriptions & payments

Purchases are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment details.

How we share data

  • Service providers that process data to make the App work: OpenAI (dish recognition), Google Firebase (analytics, authentication, backup).
  • Legal & safety: when required by law, or to detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security issues.

Other than the above, we do not share your personal data.

Data retention & deletion

We keep your data only as long as needed to provide the App. You can delete your logged data inside the App at any time, and you can request deletion of your account and associated backup by contacting us at the email below.

Children’s privacy

MeNutrition is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data, to object to or restrict its processing, and to withdraw consent. To exercise any of these, contact us at the email below.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to safeguard your information.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new “Last updated” date.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: support@menutrition.app
